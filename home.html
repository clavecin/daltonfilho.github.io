About this site

This website is a small collection of articles I wrote between 2005 and 2014, covering niche topics such as the extinct Java3D, an animated temperature map app using a Java/GeoServer/PostGIS/OpenLayers stack and a wxWidgets guide for Swing developers. I will preserve its contents on this site for those who are curious about those technologies and would like to peruse through the details. These tutorials have not been kept up to date with newer versions of libraries and languages used by them, and there's very little, if anything, that would stand up to my scrutiny in regard to development practices, not even the HTML code of this site itself. I'm still very curious about niche tech, with the only difference between me and my younger article writing self being the sheer lack of time to dive into them.
